# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We want this repository to be a welcoming place for learners and contributors.

Participants are expected to be respectful, constructive, and patient when asking questions, reviewing code, and giving feedback.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional
- Assume good intent
- Give actionable feedback
- Help learners understand, not just "fix" things for them
- Keep discussions focused on the project and learning goals

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or hateful language
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Bad-faith disruption, spam, or intimidation

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block users who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting
For conduct concerns, open a private security report if safety/privacy is involved, or contact the maintainer through the support guidance in `SUPPORT.md`.
